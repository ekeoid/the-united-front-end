The Autochef Project

The Autochef is a web application will take ingredients available from your pantry and provide suggestions to the user of possible recipes to create based on the ingredients on-hand. This requires tracking the items the users has, and matching them with recipe API’s to get a probable match. Once the suggestions of recipes are listed, the user can browse each one and identify missing ingredients, prices to fulfill recipe, and where to find the items.
